FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a typical renovation cost?
Costs vary by scope. A bathroom renovation starts at $12,000–$15,000 and can reach $35,000+. A legal basement suite ranges from $30,000–$65,000. We provide free, itemized quotes so you have a real number before any work begins.
How long does a basement finishing take?
A legal basement finishing typically takes 6–10 weeks depending on scope, permitting timelines, and size. We give you a realistic schedule during the quote phase.
Do you handle permits?
Yes. We handle all required permits including legal basement suites, structural changes, and plumbing/electrical work. Code-compliant work protects your home's value and keeps your insurance valid.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Brampton, Mississauga, Oakville, Milton, Ajax, Vaughan, Burlington, Etobicoke, Caledon, Georgetown, and surrounding GTA communities.
Are you insured?
Yes. JBD Starr carries full liability insurance and WSIB coverage for all workers. We provide proof of insurance upon request — before any work begins.
