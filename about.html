About Us

Built on Trust. Proven by Results.

For over a decade, we've been transforming GTA homes with craftsmanship, integrity, and a commitment to doing things right the first time.

Our Story

JBD Starr was founded with a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a renovation experience that's transparent, professional, and stress-free. What started as a small Brampton operation has grown into one of the GTA's most trusted renovation teams.

Over 200 projects later, we've maintained our core values — quality craftsmanship, clear communication, and treating every home as if it were our own. We don't cut corners. We don't hide costs. We deliver what we promise.

Our team is fully licensed, insured, and WSIB-covered. Every project meets Ontario Building Code standards, with permits pulled and inspections passed.
