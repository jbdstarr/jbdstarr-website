About Our Flooring Installation Service

Hardwood, LVP, tile, and carpet. We supply, deliver, and install — one team, one warranty, zero middleman. The right flooring ties every room together, and we help you choose and install the perfect option for your lifestyle and budget.

We understand that every home and every homeowner is unique. That is why we take the time to listen to your goals, assess your space, and create a customized plan that fits your budget and timeline.

What's Included

  • Hardwood & engineered wood
  • Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)
  • Ceramic & porcelain tile
  • Carpet & underpad
  • Subfloor repair & leveling
  • Material supply included
  • Heated floor system installation
  • Stair tread & riser matching
  • Baseboard & quarter-round install
  • Warranty on materials & labour
